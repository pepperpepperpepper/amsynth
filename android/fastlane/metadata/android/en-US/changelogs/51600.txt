Play-first, custom knobs, favourites & remembered state:
- Play is now the default startup screen.
- Add or remove knobs on the Play screen from a new "Knobs" picker (no more
  round-trip through Console → Pin).
- The full Console now drops a small playable keyboard into any vertical dead
  space on taller/squarer screens.
- Favourite any preset (★) and save the current sound as your own named preset;
  both show up in the searchable library under "★ Favorites" / "My Presets".
- The app now remembers your sound, pinned knobs, favourites, tuning, root note
  and mode across restarts.
